Why we making this instructions?

Currently, all major education institutions (Universities) in Hong Kong have started accepting applications for Taught Program Graduate students. For non-local students, the online application process can be complicated, especially for those who have never been counseled on applying online. Many people may miss the opportunity to enter BU to receive studies because of complicated rules and unclear order. Therefore, this website sets out the procedures for sequential application programs to help applicants who are interested in BU and reduce the probability of application failure due to the complicated procedures.

Hope It Can Help You!




Hong Kong Baptist Universitycurrently offers over 40 Taught Postgraduate Programmes in diverse disciplines with over 2,000 graduates annually. Most of the Master’s degree programmes last for 1 year in full-time mode, and 2 years in part-time mode. Each programme has its specific objectives, salient features and uniqueness.
At HKBU, we strive to provide our students with the best learning experience, emphasising student-centered learning, enriching on- and off-campus life, and the development of their global outlook. Its actualisation relies on the concerted efforts of our students and staff, our established caring culture, extensive local and global networks, and the strong research capacities.

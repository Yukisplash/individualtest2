Progremme Selection 专业选择

After activation (see Register page), return back to the System and log in. Click on "Programme" in the upper left corner to start selecting a major(see pic1).
Please pay attention to the name of the programme, the academic year and the mode of programme you want to choose, and click “Save” when it’s done. Please remember that your choice can not be changed once saved! Some majors may have a specific direction (see pic2). If it has, choose "Concentration" in the upper left corner under Programme bottom and select your first choice.
Then, you are able to entre the next step “Personal Particular”to fill in your personal information. 完成 (见上一步Register创建账号)后，返回网申系统继续登录。
点击左上角的“Programme”开始选择专业。请注意你要选择的专业名称、入学年份、授课模式，完成后点击“Save”保存，保存后不能修改。有些专业可能有特定方向，在左上角“Programme”下方选择“Concentration”选择你的第一志愿专业。

完成专业选择后，就可以进入下一步，点击“Personal Particular”进入下一步填写个人信息。

Register 账号注册

Enter the website of HKBU Graduate School, and click“Apply Now” Botton at the bottom, entering Online Application System. In the System, you shall click “Sign Up” in the upper left corner to register an account. (see from the left attaached pic)
Fill in your account information and password, click "Submit", then activate your account in the email address you filled in.(see from right attached pic)
You need to activate your account within 14 days, otherwise the account will be invalid; after successful activation, you need to submit your online application within 30 days, otherwise, the account will be invalid.
After finishing account registration, you can go on to Programme Select step.

Notes: when registering your account in the system, try not to use qq mailbox as your contact email; there is a certain risk that the school's emails will be judged as spam or overseas advertising emails by qq mailbox, it is recommended to use outlook or gmail. One account can only applying one programme. 进入香港浸会大学研究院后，点击“Apply Now”进入网上申请系统。在网申系统中，点击左上角的“Sign Up”注册账号。
填写好账号以及密码后点击“Submit”，然后在你填写的邮箱中激活账号。
你需要在14天内激活账号，否则账号会失效；成功激活后，需要在30天内提交网申，否则账号作废。
完成账号创建后，你可以进入下一步Programme Select专业选择。

Notes：在系统中注册账号时，尽量不要使用qq邮箱作为联系邮箱，学校的邮件有一定风险被qq邮箱判断为垃圾或境外广告邮件，推荐使用outlook或gmail。
